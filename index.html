Overview and Objectives

This engaging and informative lecture, based on the book "Data Deception: How to Spot Misleading Information and Avoid Being Fooled," will equip your employees with essential skills to identify, analyze, and navigate the complex world of data manipulation. Through real-life examples and interactive discussions, attendees will learn how to uncover data deception and make more informed decisions in their professional and personal lives.

Key Takeaways

  • Understanding common data manipulation techniques in graphs, visualizations, and statistical analysis
  • Identifying cognitive biases that can impact data interpretation
  • Learning how to critically evaluate data sources and visualizations
  • Acquiring practical strategies for avoiding data deception
